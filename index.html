Place Must Visit

Prithvi Cafe
i have visited in 2019

Artistic Ambience, Queue System, Artistic Decor, Pocket Friendly Menu, Outdoor Ambience, Beautiful Lighting.Served with love and care, a cuppa of Sulemani chai here is refreshing and you will be a fan. If that's not what your taste buds are accustomed to then you can try cutting chai, masala chai, ginger tea, and more there. But we suggest, do give it a shot, at least!.Be it their Aloo Paratha, their cheese chilli paratha or their mixed paranthas, you will be wowed by them. Served with dal makhani, raita, and pickle, this meal is wholesome and you will be a fan. The soft parathas are best enjoyed when still hot, and they serve you on pattals also sometimes.One of the best selling items, Keema Pav at Prithvi is packed with flavours. The consistency is good and you will be gobbling it up in no time. Plus, we bet you will be ordering extra pavs. It is quite a meal and it is quite filling. You can even try the Egg Bhurji Pav, Chicken Keema Egg Bhurji Pav, Mutton Keema Egg Bhurji Pav, and Cheese & Egg Bhurji Pav.Other things to try: Patrani Fish Fingers, Iced Teas, Sandwiches, Loaded Waffles, Nachos, Bun Maska, and refreshing shakes. Do pack some baby brownies and mini cakes on your way out!
